About BoltBNC

What sets us apart from the rest?

Features

Unlike some other bouncer providers, BoltBNC is actively maintained, and uses the latest technology to deliver streamlined performance. We are run by an ambitious group of people, with future plans for an online bouncer interface.

  • Fast - Our servers run the powerful yet swift ZNC bouncer
  • Secure - All communications with our server is encrypted with 2048-bit RSA SSL encryption and AES256. Almost all our staff have very limited rights, to protect your privacy.
  • Modern - We run the latest stable ZNC release
  • Productive - BoltBNC automates nearly everything except bouncer approval

Rules/FAQ

Thank you for using BoltBNC! Below, we answer some of your questions and ask that you follow certain rules.

  • How do I request a bouncer?

    To request a bouncer, join #BoltBNC on chat.freenode.net (Webchat). Write !request username email

  • How do I report abuse?

    We honor abuse reports. Simply ping an op'ed staff member in #BoltBNC on freenode. You can also email admin [at] this domain with reports.

  • Do you take donations?

    Yes. Click the link below to donate.


    Donations will go towards paying for fees associated with running BoltBNC. Donators that give over $5 will receive unlimited (reasonably) networks

  • Rules
    • Do not spam, troll, or otherwise break network rules
    • Comply with requests from both network and BoltBNC staff
    • Do not access blacklisted networks from BoltBNC
    • Be respectful of others while using your bouncer
    • Use common sense
  • Other Questions

    Please direct other questions to admin [at] this domain or #BoltBNC on freenode.
